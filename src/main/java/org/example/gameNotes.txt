### Rules for minesweeper ###
1. Press a square. If there is a bomb, you lose.
2. Otherwise it clears all neighbour squares up until the once that are adjacent to a bomb.
3. The squares adjacent to a or multiple bomb field's gets assigned a number that represents how many neighbours are bombs.
